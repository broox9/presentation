Porter's 5 Competitive Forces

Existing Rivalries

Medium. No direct competitors, but substitutes are currently very popular. Bibles include YouVersion, Olive Tree gloBible, Logos, and many more. Churches often you use Facebook or Twitter to post information, as well as their websites

Threat of New Entrants

Very high due to low cost of entry into the market and a good amount of people with domain knowledge of the customer.

Supplier Power

Low. Hosting, developers, and smart phones are all in competitive markets themselves.

Buyer Power

High/Very High, especially initially. Existing rivalry is medium and threat of substitute is high so a balance of pricing and convenience must be found.

Threat of Substitutes

High. Churches use social media sites like Facebook and twitter currently, and there are a lot of bible apps on the market today.
