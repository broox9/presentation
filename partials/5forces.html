Competitive Forces

Threat of New Entrants

Very high due to low cost of entry into the market and abundance of people with domain knowledge of the customer

Supplier Power

Low. Hosting, developers, and smart phones are all in competitive markets themselves.

Buyer Power

High/Very High, especially initially. Existing rivalry is high and threat of substitute is high so a balance of pricing and convenience must be found.

Threat of Substitues

High. Lo/No-tech methods like hand counting, writing down, taking mental inventory, data entry into spreadsheets, etc are used today. The threat of other non-target software being used for this purpose also exists.

Existing Rivalries

High. Lo/No-tech methods like hand counting, writing down, taking mental inventory, data entry into spreadsheets, etc are used today. The threat of other non-target software being used for this purpose also exists.
