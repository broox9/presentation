A Tale of Two Users

Exposition

Pastor Chris wants to reinforce his messages as well as send the church information so he signs up for MyChurchBible.com and promotes it’s bible app to the church.

Tina hears of the MyChurchBible App at church and subscribes to her church’s feed since she uses her iPad for a bible already anyway.

Rising Action

Pastor Chris starts posting his upcoming launch scripture and reference scriptures to MyChurchBible for his members to see. He also adds a survey post and an announcement for an upcoming clothing drive. All this information is also posted to Facebook and Twitter.

Rising Action

Tina adjusts her privacy settings because she doesn’t want to feel like every little thing she does in the app will be watched.

Climax

Tina During her devotional time Tina decides to read the scriptures Pastor Chris posted and it helps her to remember and apply the message. She also sees the announcement.

Falling Action

Pastor Chris can view his MyChurchBible.com dashboard and see how many subscribers his church has, what type of engagement his posts get (both in app and on social media), check-ins, how much time people spend using the bible app, feedback, prayer requests, and much more.

Denouement

After church serves the bible app prompts Tina for surveys from time to time and she fills them out sometimes.
