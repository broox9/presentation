Needs

Church

    Churches want to provide their members with the tools, guidance and information to foster a personal relationship with God Churches want to engage members on more than just Sunday, for point #1 and also to build a sense of community Churches want feedback from members and attendees Churches want to promote and reinforce the content from their worship services and classes Churches want to disseminate information to their members like announcements, devotionals, etc Churches want information on participation

Members

    Members want a custom level of engagement and privacy Members want a relationship with God Members want some level (usually a low level) of direction Members want to hear the message Members want a voice

Features

    Full, linkable Bible with multiple translations Content feed for churches to post to and members to subscribe to Check Ins Multiple Post types Scripture Posts feature one or more scriptures that link back to the Bible portion of the App Survey Posts to give and receive feedback Link Posts for sharing links to audio/video and other content

Churches

    Affordable Web/Mobile Dashboard for viewing analytics and posting content Church Profile for members to subscribe to Push to App feed as well as social media feeds Mobile App flags/push notifications Analytics on every action, personally identifiable if the user chooses Schedule Posts for specific days/times

Members

    Custom user privacy settings so that users share the level of personally identifiable information that they want User profiles and subscriptions that are not locked to a specific church Prayer request and feedback forms

Benefits

    Information that is normally on a church website can be pushed to members via mobile app The Benefits of a custom app without the cost Bible portion of the app can be used during service and turned to the launch scripture for the member 2 points to give and receive feedback allow for receiving different kinds of feedback (After Service survey form and Anytime Feedback from)

    Church

    Engagement Analytics on every action, whether personally identifiable or not Scheduled Posts allow Content to be pre-planned Send out message reinforcement, announcements, events notifications, devotionals, and other information to subscribers at anytime

    Member

    Users tie personal information to the actions they take at their discretion Users have access to their church’s curated content on demand Users have a voice in feedback forms, surveys, and prayer request forms Users don’t have to wait for the opportune time to ask a question or request information
